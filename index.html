Who Are We?

We are a small ice cream shop located in New Haven, Connecticut. Our products come from locally sourced dairy farms in New Haven county! Half of all of our profits go to various charities throughout the state of Connecticut!


We sell a wide array of dairy products including ice cream cones, bowls, and more. We even sell special ice cream for pets!


The Chubby Cow was founded in February of 2022 by Emma Adams and Mari Arnold.
